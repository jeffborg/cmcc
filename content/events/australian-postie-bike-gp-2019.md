The Australian Postie Bike GP was presented as a family-friendly team race held on closed streets around the Cessnock CBD.

The original public page outlined:

- sign on and scrutineering on Saturday 9 November 2019
- race day on Sunday 10 November 2019
- team-based racing on Honda CT110 Postie Bikes
- a course through central Cessnock and the TAFE precinct
