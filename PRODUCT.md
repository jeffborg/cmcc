# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local club members and their families who use the site to find meeting times, browse upcoming events and results, view news and photo galleries, and discover how to join or volunteer. Editors (committee members) also use the repo/Pages CMS to keep content up to date.

## Product Purpose

Provide a clear, low-friction public presence for Cessnock Motor Cycle Club that preserves legacy content, surfaces event and membership information, and makes it easy for members and the public to find contact and committee details. Success looks like fewer content support requests, up-to-date event listings, and straightforward next steps for joining or volunteering.

## Brand Personality

Welcoming, community-focused, approachable

## Anti-references

- Not corporate or high-gloss marketing sites
- Not aggressive or hyper-stylised extreme-sports aesthetics
- Avoid trendy SaaS conventions (hero-metric templates, gradient text, neon-on-black)

## Design Principles

- Content-first: information (events, membership, contact) must be immediately findable and scannable.
- Community-first imagery: use real club photos and preserved legacy media to convey history and belonging.
- Clear affordances: large, readable type, clear link/buttons for membership and event actions.
- Maintainability: design must be easy to update via Pages CMS and simple static rebuilds.
- Accessible: meet WCAG AA standards and provide reduced-motion and clear focus states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Ensure good color contrast, alt text on images, keyboard navigability, and support for reduced motion preferences.
